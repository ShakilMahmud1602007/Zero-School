My Childhood

I grew up in a village in Bangladesh. Where in the morning I could smell the smell of cow dung. There was a river named ‘Boral’ and, I had to cross the river though bridge for going school. The wind of that river was so smooth which always touched me. Though that bridge is replaced by a new one, but my memory of childhood still stuck in the old one.
